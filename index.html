More about me

Hey! I'm Remy, an avatar of organised chaos.

I've been playing video games since I was old enough to walk and started out playing Sonic the Hedgehog on the Sega MEGADRIVE.

I grew up on consoles throughout my childhood and obsessed deeply over platformers, RTS and RPG style games until I was old enough to play online when I received my first internet capable computer at 13, and started playing MMOs and online shooters.

I've never been one for serious competition, mostly because:

  1. I Suck and
  2. Have the attention span of a squirrel in a park full of nuts.

But that hasn't stopped me from having a good laugh and some solid plays in games like CS:GO, Call of Duty, Battlefield, or my current go-to for angry russian bulletstorms, Escape from Tarkov.

I also play a range of other games from various genres, and have a deep and abiding love of space simulators and Sci-fi games.

Currently I work as an Engineer and enjoy my weekends gaming, and this is where I decided to take up streaming so others can enjoy my personal brand of chaos and the occasional serious game.
